DISCOVER APEX BUILD

WHAT WE DO

Complete Architecture, Civil, & Build Capabilities

From conceptual design blueprints to steel fabrication and final landscaping, we execute turn-key construction management.

Residential

Residential Construction

Luxury modern estates, custom architectural villas, and premium multi-family housing complexes crafted to the highest finishes.

Residential Construction Details

We build luxury residential properties with high focus on customized engineering. Our services include planning, soil exploration, foundation design, framing, structural masonry, insulation, luxury waterproofing, interior fitting, and smart home pre-wiring.

  • Custom Premium Mansions & Villas
  • Sustainable Eco-Framing & Concrete Pods
  • Advanced Acoustic & Thermal Insulation
  • 10-Year Structural Handover Guarantee
Commercial

Commercial Buildings

Corporate offices, urban high-rises, retail complexes, and hotel towers optimized for structural energy efficiency and workspace flow.

Commercial Buildings Details

Apex Build handles large-scale commercial developments. We provide pre-construction cost modeling, structural steel fabrication, high-load glass facades, safety exit compliance, state-of-the-art HVAC integration, and customized interior office partitioning.

  • Steel-Frame Office Skyscrapers
  • Reinforced Concrete Retail Centers
  • Acoustic Zoning & Fire Suppression
  • LEED Energy Star Accreditation Audits
Industrial

Industrial Construction

Advanced warehousing, cold storage systems, assembly plants, and manufacturing units that demand heavy floor-loading and high clearances.

Industrial Construction Details

Heavy industry requires durable structural engineering. We deliver heavy foundation excavation, reinforced concrete slabs with epoxy seals, crane rails, logistics bays, high-voltage panel routing, and custom steel roof spans.

  • Smart Distribution & Logistics Hubs
  • Heavy Machinery Foundations
  • Specialized Environmental Containment
  • High-Span Steel Portal Frames
Interior

Interior Design & Fit-Out

Bespoke luxury interior designs, custom millwork, ergonomic ceilings, and lighting layouts that elevate corporate and residential spaces.

Interior Design Details

Our premium interior fitout team works with premium stone, hardwoods, custom metals, and acoustic panels. We cover ceiling detailing, custom lighting layout, bespoke furniture integration, and fine finishing works.

  • Executive Corporate Lounges & Boardrooms
  • Luxury Marble & Hardwood Installation
  • Custom Floating Ceilings with Integrated LEDs
  • Ergonomic Space-Planning & Furnishing
Renovation

Renovation & Retrofitting

Structural strengthening, historic facade preservation, and modernizing older commercial or residential buildings to present safety codes.

Renovation & Retrofitting Details

We revitalize older buildings. Our crew handles structural column reinforcement, seismological retrofitting, energy-efficient window/facade upgrades, concrete repairing, and electrical rewiring.

  • Historic Building Preservation
  • Seismic Column & Beam Retrofitting
  • HVAC & Insulation Modernization
  • Interior and Facade Overhauls
Architecture

Architecture & Drafting

Stunning conceptual design sketches, 3D renderings, and precise engineering blueprints that balance aesthetic beauty with code compliance.

Architecture & Drafting Details

Apex Build bridges the gap between artistic vision and building realities. We utilize BIM (Building Information Modeling) and VR (Virtual Reality) walkthroughs to draft structural layouts, facade detailing, and local zoning approvals.

  • Aesthetic & Structural Feasibility Designs
  • 3D VR Client Render Previews
  • BIM Coordination & Space Optimization
  • Zoning, Code Compliance, & Permit Processing
Civil

Civil Engineering

Foundation piling, bridges, custom retaining walls, drainage grids, and municipal concrete systems engineered for extreme load conditions.

Civil Engineering Details

Our heavy civil division supports critical infrastructure works. We design deep soil piling, heavy retaining structures, high-performance storm drainage grids, and site earthworks grading.

  • Deep Pier Piling & Site Stabilization
  • Stormwater Management & Drainage Grids
  • High-Load Retaining Walls & Abutments
  • Municipal Infrastructure Partnership Projects
Management

Project Management

Turnkey construction supervision, procurement oversight, scheduling audit, sub-contractor control, and site safety management.

Project Management Details

We take complete responsibility from zoning to handover. Our managers use Primavera P6 and MS Project to monitor material procurement pipelines, safety enforcement, budget variances, and stakeholder reports.

  • Primavera P6 Critical Path Scheduling
  • Subcontractor Auditing & Coordination
  • Detailed Cost Control & Procurement Audits
  • OSHA Standard Site Safety Supervision
